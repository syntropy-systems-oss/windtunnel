# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Wind Tunnel is committed to a professional, welcoming, and constructive
community. Participation is open to people of every background and experience
level who engage in good faith.

Expected behavior includes:

- discussing ideas and code rather than attacking people;
- giving and receiving specific, respectful feedback;
- acknowledging mistakes and helping repair their impact;
- respecting privacy, boundaries, and differing experiences; and
- crediting the work and ideas of others.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate
intimidation, sexualized attention, publishing private information without
permission, sustained disruption, and other conduct that would be inappropriate
in a professional setting.

## Scope

This policy applies in project repositories, issue trackers, reviews, and other
project spaces. It also applies when someone is officially representing Wind
Tunnel or Syntropy Systems in connection with the project.

## Reporting and enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove content, close or lock discussions,
reject contributions, or temporarily or permanently restrict participation when
needed to protect the community.

To report a concern, contact a maintainer listed in
[`.github/CODEOWNERS`](.github/CODEOWNERS). If the report must remain private,
open an issue containing no sensitive details and ask for a private contact, or
use [GitHub's abuse-reporting channel](https://support.github.com/contact/report-abuse).
Reports will be handled as privately, promptly, and fairly as circumstances
allow. Retaliation against a reporter or participant in an investigation is not
permitted.
